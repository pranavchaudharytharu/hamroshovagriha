A comprehensive care home for seniors

Hamro Shova Griha is a comprehensive care home with specialized day care service residential care, home care service on demand.

ABOUT US

We, Hamro Shova Griha, situated at Sunakoti, Lalitpur , help in providing a quality and respectful living for senior citizens with the help of qualified professionals, with a dream to nurture each others growth in a spirit of commitment, trust and transparency. We create a friendly, caring and family like atmosphere for the elderly people, where they can share their joys and sorrows and live happily.
